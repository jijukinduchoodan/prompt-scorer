How to improve

    What we measure

    Each dimension is scored from 0–20 using transparent pattern checks — no signup, and nothing leaves your browser.

    • 🎯

      Specificity

      Concrete details, numbers, examples, and clear asks.

    • 📋

      Context

      Background, audience, goals, and situation for the model.

    • 🎭

      Role Assignment

      An expert persona or role that shapes the response style.

    • 📐

      Output Format

      Structure such as JSON, tables, bullets, or labeled sections.

    • 📏

      Constraints

      Limits, must/don’t rules, tone bounds, and length caps.
